Minggu 13 memperluas kerangka strategi ke dimensi relational—nilai yang diciptakan via relationships dengan stakeholders, tidak hanya via products atau markets. Dua teori komplementer:

Firms that enhance the capabilities of their stakeholders—not merely extract value from them—build cooperative advantages that prove far more durable than purely competitive ones. Adaptasi dari Westermann-Behaylo, Van Buren III & Berman — Business Horizons (2016)

Tema 1 — Stakeholder Capability Enhancement (Westermann-Behaylo et al. 2016)

Mengintegrasikan Sen's capability approach (Nobel Economics 1998) dengan stakeholder theory. Capability adalah kemampuan seseorang untuk mencapai functionings yang bernilai—bukan sekadar income. Perusahaan yang meningkatkan capability stakeholder (employees, suppliers, community) menciptakan:

  • Human dignity—stakeholder hidup dengan lebih bermartabat.
  • Cooperative advantage—relational capital yang sulit direplikasi competitor.

Tema 2 — Engagement sebagai Sumber Competitive Advantage (Kumar & Pansari 2016)

Engagement melampaui satisfaction. Empat pillars:

  1. Customer engagement: emotional + behavioral connection dengan brand.
  2. Employee engagement: discretionary effort + commitment.
  3. Stakeholder engagement: formal dialog dengan suppliers, partners, NGOs.
  4. Societal engagement: community impact, CSR strategis.

Perusahaan high-engagement secara konsisten outperform low-engagement di berbagai metrik finansial dan non-finansial.

Mengapa Topik Ini Relevan untuk UAS

Dua artikel ini menyediakan advanced frameworks yang melengkapi kerangka klasik (Porter, Barney) dengan perspektif yang lebih modern dan sesuai konteks Indonesia. Banyak perusahaan Indonesia—terutama platform businesses (GoTo, Tokopedia), perusahaan yang bergantung stakeholder network (Astra, Salim), dan BUMN dengan mandat nasional—dapat dianalisis dengan lebih baik melalui lens stakeholder & engagement ini.

Stakeholder Theory — Fondasi + Salience Model

Foundation (Freeman 1984)

R. Edward Freeman dalam Strategic Management: A Stakeholder Approach (1984) mendefinisikan stakeholder sebagai:

Any group or individual who can affect or is affected by the achievement of the organization's objectives. R. Edward Freeman — Strategic Management: A Stakeholder Approach (1984)

Implikasi radikal di era itu: manajemen bertanggung jawab kepada lebih dari hanya shareholders. Perusahaan adalah nexus of relationships dengan banyak kelompok:

Primary Stakeholders

Kelompok yang direct exchange dengan perusahaan—kehilangan mereka akan mengancam survival:

  • Shareholders (modal finansial)
  • Employees (tenaga kerja)
  • Customers (revenue)
  • Suppliers (input)
  • Communities (license to operate, workforce, market)

Secondary Stakeholders

Kelompok yang influence perusahaan meski tidak direct exchange:

  • Media (shape public perception)
  • NGOs / activist groups (campaign)
  • Government & regulators (regulate)
  • Trade unions (collective bargaining)
  • Academic / research communities

Stakeholder Salience Model (Mitchell, Agle & Wood 1997)

Tidak semua stakeholder dilayani dengan intensitas sama—manajemen harus prioritize. Mitchell, Agle & Wood mengusulkan kerangka dengan tiga atribut:

Gambar 13.1 — Stakeholder Salience Model (Mitchell, Agle & Wood 1997)

POWER LEGITIMACY URGENCY

1. Dormant (power only)

2. Discretionary (legitimacy only)

3. Demanding (urgency only)

4. Dominant (P+L)

5. Dangerous (P+U)

6. Dependent (L+U)

7. DEFINITIVE (P+L+U)

Tiga Atribut

Power

Kemampuan untuk mempengaruhi perusahaan mencapai/gagal mencapai tujuan. Power dapat datang dari: coercive (kekuatan fisik/legal), utilitarian (resources kontrol), atau normative (prestige/moral authority).

Contoh Indonesia: OJK untuk bank (regulatory power), major shareholders dengan kepemilikan mayoritas, journalist senior yang dapat mempengaruhi publik opinion.

Legitimacy

Persepsi bahwa klaim stakeholder adalah proper, desirable, or appropriate—socially atau morally valid. Legitimacy bersumber dari norms, values, beliefs.

Contoh Indonesia: karyawan memiliki claim legitimate atas wages yang adil. Komunitas sekitar tambang memiliki claim legitimate atas dampak lingkungan.

Urgency

Klaim yang time-sensitive atau critical—membutuhkan immediate attention. Dua dimensi: time-sensitivity (cepat vs. lambat) dan criticality (serius vs. minor).

Contoh Indonesia: krisis lingkungan yang memerlukan immediate response (tumpahan minyak, banjir akibat tambang). Karyawan yang mogok menuntut kenaikan upah.

Tujuh Tipe Stakeholder

Kombinasi 3 atribut menghasilkan 7 tipe:

Low Salience (satu atribut)

  • Dormant (power only): punya kekuatan tetapi tidak aktif atau tanpa legitimacy. Management watch but low priority.
  • Discretionary (legitimacy only): klaim legitimate tetapi tanpa power untuk memaksa. Often recipients of voluntary CSR.
  • Demanding (urgency only): vocal, time-sensitive claim tetapi tanpa power atau legitimacy. Noise tetapi low salience actual.

Moderate Salience (dua atribut)

  • Dominant (power + legitimacy): formal stakeholders yang berhak dengan kekuatan. Shareholders, employees, government. Focal management attention.
  • Dangerous (power + urgency): aggressive claims dengan power tetapi questionable legitimacy. Activist investors, hostile acquirers, protestors yang extreme.
  • Dependent (legitimacy + urgency): legitimate urgent claims tetapi tanpa power. Depend pada advocates untuk representasi. Misalnya korban bencana lingkungan.

High Salience (tiga atribut)

  • Definitive (power + legitimacy + urgency): top priority untuk management attention. Stakeholders yang memerlukan immediate response dengan kekuatan dan hak yang valid.

Aplikasi Praktis

Stakeholder salience model membantu management:

  1. **Prioritize attention dan resources—tidak semua stakeholder dengan intensitas sama.
  2. **Identify strategic risk—stakeholders yang akan bergeser ke "definitive" dapat di-anticipate.
  3. **Design engagement strategy—different tipe memerlukan different approaches.
  4. **Allocate CSR investment—discretionary stakeholders are often subjects CSR; definitive stakeholders require strategic attention.

Stakeholder Capability Enhancement — Westermann-Behaylo et al. 2016

Sen's Capability Approach (Foundation)

Amartya Sen (Nobel Economics 1998) dan Martha Nussbaum mengembangkan capability approach sebagai alternative untuk utilitarian (hanya hitung utility total) dan Rawlsian (hanya hitung primary goods) frameworks.

Inti Capability Approach

Kesejahteraan manusia tidak hanya diukur dari pendapatan atau resources. Yang penting adalah kemampuan (capabilities) seseorang untuk mencapai functionings (actual achievements/doings/beings) yang mereka nilai.

Contoh: dua orang dengan income sama dapat memiliki capability berbeda karena perbedaan kesehatan, akses pendidikan, kondisi lingkungan sosial, kebebasan politik, dll.

Fokus: real freedoms untuk hidup kehidupan yang dinilai.

Integration dengan Stakeholder Theory (Westermann-Behaylo et al.)

Westermann-Behaylo, Van Buren III & Berman (2016) mengusulkan integrasi:

Kritik terhadap Stakeholder Theory Klasik

Freeman's stakeholder theory (1984) mengatakan: "balance interests of all stakeholders". Tetapi apa artinya "interest"? Stakeholder theory tradisional sering collapse interest menjadi ekonomis—higher wages, fair prices, community investment.

Westermann-Behaylo et al. argue: interest seharusnya difokuskan pada capability—bukan sekadar output ekonomi, tetapi kemampuan untuk hidup kehidupan yang bermartabat.

Konsep Baru: Stakeholder Capability Enhancement

Perusahaan dapat meng-enhance capabilities stakeholder dengan:

  • Skills training untuk employees—bukan hanya untuk job tetapi untuk career progression.
  • Supplier development programs—helping SME suppliers upgrade capabilities.
  • Customer literacy programs—financial literacy untuk bank customers, digital skills untuk platform users.
  • Community investment dalam education, health, infrastructure—bukan donasi tetapi capability-building.
Enhancement ini bukan CSR generik—ia specific improvements pada kemampuan stakeholder untuk pursue lives they value.

Cooperative Advantage

Definisi

Cooperative advantage adalah value created through enhanced stakeholder capabilities yang membuat perusahaan dan stakeholders bersama-sama better off. Bukan zero-sum (one's gain is another's loss)—tetapi positive-sum cooperation.

Cooperative vs. Competitive Advantage

Competitive advantage: value yang dihasilkan despite atau against competitors. Focus: market positioning, cost/differentiation vs rivals.

Cooperative advantage: value yang dihasilkan with stakeholders. Focus: relationships, trust, capability building, shared value creation.

Banyak perusahaan strong di competitive advantage tetapi weak di cooperative advantage—atau sebaliknya. Best-in-class firms strong di keduanya.

Durability

Cooperative advantage sering lebih sulit direplikasi dari competitive advantage:

  • Technology can be copied; trust relationships cannot.
  • Low-cost manufacturing can be imitated; decade of supplier development cannot be replayed overnight.
  • Product features can be matched; community reputation cannot.

Contoh: Unilever Sustainable Living Plan (USLP)

Unilever Indonesia (Minggu 10 sudah dibahas sebagai shared value). Dari lens stakeholder capability enhancement:

Smallholder Farmer Programs

Unilever tidak hanya beli teh atau palm oil dari smallholders—mereka investment dalam agricultural training, sustainability certification, farm management tools. Hasil: farmers meningkat capability (higher yield, better prices, sustainable practices). Unilever mendapat: supply chain yang lebih reliable dan certified sustainable.

Cooperative advantage: trust relationships dengan ribuan smallholder tersebar—tidak dapat direplikasi oleh new entrant dengan kapital saja.

Saraswati Women Entrepreneurs

Program untuk mengembangkan women micro-entrepreneurs as direct-to-consumer distributors di daerah rural. Unilever memberikan training, kredit mikro, product access. Hasil: women gain economic capability + independence; Unilever mendapat reach ke konsumen yang tidak terjangkau formal retail.

Aplikasi Broader ke Indonesia

Gojek Driver Ecosystem

Digitalisasi driver ojek dari informal ke formal economic actors. Driver gain: income tracking, identity, access to insurance, training programs, financial services (tabungan, pinjaman). Gojek gain: reliable driver pool, brand asosiasi positive, cooperative advantage vs competitors.

Pertanyaan kritis (untuk diskusi): apakah ini genuine capability enhancement atau sophisticated form of gig exploitation? Debat aktif—Minggu 13 adalah venue yang tepat untuk exploration mendalam.

BRI UMKM Financial Inclusion

BRI tidak hanya lending ke UMKM—mereka invest dalam financial literacy, business management training, digital onboarding (BRILink agents). UMKM gain: access to formal finance + capabilities untuk grow. BRI gain: large UMKM portfolio, lower NPL (due to training), customer loyalty. Classic cooperative advantage.

Astra Community Programs

Astra beroperasi di banyak komunitas lokal via subsidiaries. Programs seperti vocational schools (SMK Astra), road safety education, environmental programs. Community gain: jobs, skills, infrastructure. Astra gain: license to operate, reliable local workforce, community support saat crisis.

Implikasi untuk Strategic Management

Westermann-Behaylo et al. mengusulkan strategic management yang lebih human-centered:

  1. **Stakeholder analysis tidak hanya "whose interests"—tetapi "whose capabilities can we enhance".
  2. **Metrics tidak hanya financial—tetapi measure actual capability improvements (skills learned, economic mobility achieved, health improvements).
  3. **Horizon tidak hanya short-term ROI—tetapi multi-generational impact (youth today become productive adults tomorrow).

Ini challenging untuk perusahaan listed dengan pressure quarterly earnings—tetapi ini yang membedakan firms yang build durable cooperative advantage dari yang hanya compete zero-sum.

Competitive Advantage through Engagement — Kumar & Pansari 2016

V. Kumar & Anita Pansari mengusulkan kerangka four pillars of engagement sebagai sumber sustainable competitive advantage. Argumen inti: engagement berbeda dari satisfaction—dan perusahaan dengan high engagement outperform di semua dimensi kinerja.

Satisfaction vs. Engagement

Satisfaction

"Saya puas dengan pembelian/pekerjaan/relationship terakhir." Passive. Dapat diukur setelah transaction. Hygiene factor—diperlukan tetapi tidak sufficient.

Engagement

"Saya aktif terhubung dengan brand/perusahaan—emotionally, behaviorally, beyond transactional interactions." Active. Termasuk behaviors seperti recommending (word-of-mouth), defending brand publicly, providing feedback, co-creating products, building community dengan brand.

Four Pillars

Gambar 13.2 — Four Pillars of Engagement (Kumar & Pansari 2016)

Pillar 1 — Customer Engagement

Components

Kumar & Pansari identifikasi customer engagement components:

  • Customer Value: direct transactional value.
  • Customer Influence: word-of-mouth, peer recommendations, social media advocacy.
  • Customer Knowledge Contribution: feedback, suggestions, co-creation.
  • Customer Referrals: proactive bringing new customers.
Engaged customers generate Customer Engagement Value (CEV) yang melampaui Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) tradisional.

Business Impact

High customer engagement correlates dengan:

  • Lower customer acquisition cost (organic referrals).
  • Higher customer lifetime value.
  • Better product-market fit via feedback loops.
  • Resilience selama crisis (loyal customers defend brand).

Pillar 2 — Employee Engagement

Components

Engaged employees menunjukkan:

  • Discretionary effort: giving extra beyond contractual.
  • Emotional commitment: caring about company success.
  • Cognitive engagement: focus, concentration, innovation.
  • Behavioral: lower absenteeism, intent to stay.

Business Impact

Gallup research (multi-year, multi-industry): companies with high employee engagement show:

  • Higher productivity (~20% higher than median).
  • Lower turnover (40-60% lower).
  • Better customer ratings.
  • Higher profitability.

Pillar 3 — Stakeholder Engagement

Scope

Beyond customers and employees: suppliers, partners, government, NGOs, media, academia. Formal engagement through:

  • Supplier relationship management programs.
  • Strategic partnerships dengan transparency.
  • Regulatory dialog that is proactive (not just compliance).
  • NGO partnerships untuk complex societal challenges.

Business Impact

Stakeholder engagement enables:

  • Supply chain resilience.
  • Risk management (early warning via engagement with critics).
  • License to operate di community dan regulasi.
  • Innovation via co-creation dengan partners.

Pillar 4 — Societal Engagement

Scope

Contribution ke society broadly:

  • Strategic CSR (bukan philanthropy ad-hoc).
  • Community investment in education, health, infrastructure.
  • Environmental commitment (ESG alignment).
  • Public policy engagement for systemic issues.

Business Impact

Societal engagement correlates dengan:

  • Stronger brand positioning (especially for younger demographics).
  • Better talent attraction (purpose-driven).
  • Investor favor (ESG-focused capital increasing).
  • Lower regulatory risk.

Integration — The Engagement Ecosystem

Kumar & Pansari argue bahwa 4 pillars saling memperkuat:

  • Employee engagement drives customer engagement (engaged employees serve customers better).
  • Customer engagement drives stakeholder engagement (loyal customers willing to partner).
  • Stakeholder engagement drives societal engagement (suppliers + NGOs = community impact).
  • Societal engagement drives employee engagement (purpose-driven workers).

Perusahaan yang strong di 4 pillars membuat engagement ecosystem yang mutually reinforcing dan sulit direplikasi.

Measurement

Kumar & Pansari usulkan Engagement Index—composite score yang measures engagement across 4 pillars. Indices yang populer:

  • Employee: Gallup Q12, Utrecht Work Engagement Scale.
  • Customer: Net Promoter Score (NPS), Customer Effort Score, Engagement metrics.
  • Stakeholder: Supplier Satisfaction Index, Partnership Quality Index.
  • Societal: ESG ratings, community impact metrics, brand purpose indices.

Perusahaan yang regularly measure + act pada 4 pillars outperform peers.

Pemeriksa Konsep · Stakeholder & Engagement

K4. Cooperative advantage vs. competitive advantage: bedakan konsep ini dengan contoh Indonesia. Mana yang lebih sustainable?

K5. BCA dikenal memiliki customer loyalty yang tinggi. Apakah ini engagement (Kumar-Pansari) atau sekedar satisfaction? Apa bukti-bukti yang akan Anda cari untuk memvalidasi klaim engagement?
