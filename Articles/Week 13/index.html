Analisis Artikel 26 — Westermann-Behaylo, Van Buren III & Berman (2016)

Judul lengkap: Stakeholder Capability Enhancement as a Path to Promote Human Dignity and Cooperative Advantage. Penulis: Michelle K. Westermann-Behaylo, Harry J. Van Buren III, Shawn L. Berman. Publikasi: Business Horizons, Vol. 59 No. 1 (Jan-Feb 2016), pp. 79–88. Tipe riset: konseptual—integrasi teoritis.

Catatan Sumber Primer

PDF artikel tidak disediakan. Analisis disusun dari abstrak publik + literatur kanonik tentang capability approach (Sen 1999, 2009; Nussbaum 2011), stakeholder theory (Freeman 1984; Donaldson & Preston 1995; Mitchell et al. 1997), dan cooperative strategy (Dyer & Singh 1998). Mahasiswa dianjurkan mencari PDF asli untuk pendalaman.

Pertanyaan Riset / Kontribusi

Bagaimana perusahaan dapat mengintegrasikan ethical imperative untuk menghormati human dignity dengan strategic imperative untuk menciptakan competitive advantage? Artikel ini mengusulkan kerangka integratif: stakeholder capability enhancement sebagai path yang simultaneously promote human dignity DAN menghasilkan cooperative advantage.

Background Teoretis

1. Sen's Capability Approach

Amartya Sen (Nobel Economics 1998) mengembangkan capability approach sebagai alternative untuk utilitarian (sum of utility) dan resource-based (Rawlsian primary goods) conceptions of welfare. Inti: kesejahteraan manusia diukur dari capabilities (freedoms to achieve) bukan dari resources atau utility.

Nussbaum (2011) extend ini dengan list of central capabilities: life, bodily health, bodily integrity, senses-imagination-thought, emotions, practical reason, affiliation, other species, play, control over environment.

2. Stakeholder Theory

Freeman (1984): stakeholders adalah entities yang can affect or are affected by organization. Donaldson & Preston (1995): stakeholder theory memiliki tiga dimensi—descriptive, instrumental, normative. Berman et al. (1999): stakeholder management dapat bersumber dari strategic concerns (instrumental) atau moral commitments (normative).

3. Cooperative Strategy

Dyer & Singh (1998) memperkenalkan relational view: competitive advantage dapat bersumber dari relationships dengan specific partners (supply chain partners, alliances, customers). Ini menambah lens beyond industry-based (Porter) dan resource-based (Barney).

Argumen Utama

Westermann-Behaylo et al. mengintegrasikan ketiga tradisi untuk argue:

Proposisi 1 — Enhanced Capabilities = Enhanced Human Dignity

Human dignity bukan konsep abstrak—ia concrete ketika orang memiliki capabilities to pursue valued functionings. Perusahaan yang enhances stakeholder capabilities (skills training, economic opportunity, health access, dsb.) secara langsung promote human dignity.

Proposisi 2 — Enhanced Capabilities = Cooperative Advantage

Stakeholder dengan enhanced capabilities menjadi partners yang lebih capable: suppliers yang lebih produktif, employees yang lebih skilled, customers yang lebih loyal, communities yang lebih supportive. Relationships yang terbentuk melalui capability enhancement menciptakan cooperative advantage—value yang co-created yang sulit direplikasi.

Proposisi 3 — Human Dignity and Cooperative Advantage are Reinforcing

Tidak ada trade-off. Promote human dignity via capability enhancement = menciptakan cooperative advantage. Ini berbeda dari framing "CSR vs profit" yang sering zero-sum. Cooperative advantage adalah outcome natural dari genuine stakeholder capability enhancement.

Framework Implementasi

Step 1 — Identify Stakeholders dan Capabilities yang Relevant

Untuk setiap stakeholder group, identifikasi: apa capabilities yang mereka perlukan untuk functionings yang bernilai? Employees: skills, health, professional development. Suppliers: business skills, technology access, working capital. Customers: product literacy, financial capability. Communities: infrastructure, education, health.

Step 2 — Assess Current State vs. Potential

Apa gap antara current capabilities dan potential? Di mana perusahaan dapat contribute secara meaningful?

Step 3 — Design Capability Enhancement Programs

Specific programs yang address gaps:

  • Training programs untuk employees (bukan hanya job-specific tetapi career development).
  • Supplier development programs (training, certification, working capital).
  • Customer education programs (financial literacy, product usage, digital skills).
  • Community infrastructure investment.

Step 4 — Measure Actual Capability Improvements

Bukan inputs (money spent) tetapi outputs (capabilities gained). Metrics tangible: skills learned, businesses upgraded, income mobility achieved, health improvements, education completed.

Step 5 — Integrate dengan Business Strategy

Capability enhancement tidak side-program—ia integral dengan supply chain strategy, HR strategy, customer engagement strategy, community relations. Terintegrasi = cooperative advantage; terpisah = charity/compliance.

Kontribusi Teoretis

1. Mengintegrasikan Ethical + Strategic

Banyak literatur strategi treats ethics sebagai constraint (apa yang harus dihindari) atau external CSR (philanthropy). Artikel ini mengintegrasikan ethics ke strategi core.

2. Memperkenalkan Cooperative Advantage sebagai Kategori

Selain competitive advantage (Porter) dan resource-based advantage (Barney), cooperative advantage adalah kategori ketiga—value dari relationships. Konsisten dengan Dyer & Singh relational view.

3. Memberi Operational Framework

Capability approach Sen sering dikritik terlalu abstrak untuk management. Artikel ini operationalisasi ke management decisions.

Keterbatasan

  • **Konseptual, bukan empiris: belum ada measurement validated untuk capability enhancement. Perusahaan harus develop sendiri metrics.
  • **Complex implementation: identifying relevant capabilities per stakeholder group is resource-intensive.
  • **Long time horizon: capability enhancement takes years—returns on investment bisa tertunda—tidak cocok dengan quarterly earnings pressure.

Aplikasi ke Konteks Indonesia

Gojek Driver Ecosystem — Case Study

Pra-Gojek: driver ojek informal dengan limited capabilities—income unstable, tidak ada identitas formal, tidak ada insurance, tidak ada career path.

Post-Gojek: driver dengan formal identity (registered), income tracking, access to insurance, financial services (tabungan via GoPay), peer community, training programs (customer service, safety).

Evaluasi capability enhancement:

  • ✅ Digital literacy: drivers belajar menggunakan apps, manage ratings.
  • ✅ Financial literacy: tracking income, savings, credit history.
  • ✅ Health & safety: insurance coverage, safety training.
  • ⚠️ Independence: driver tergantung pada algorithm + platform policies.
  • ⚠️ Wage: meskipun income tracking ada, actual earnings masih volatile.
Gojek memang meningkatkan capability drivers dalam beberapa dimensi; tetapi juga menciptakan new dependencies. Debat akademis aktif apakah ini net capability enhancement atau sophisticated labor extraction.

BRI UMKM Ecosystem

BRI tidak hanya lending—invest dalam UMKM capability enhancement: financial literacy, business management training, digital tools (BRILink), market access. Clear net capability enhancement, cooperative advantage yang manifest dalam UMKM portfolio yang loyal dan NPL rendah.

Unilever Smallholder Programs

Sustainable sourcing yang tidak hanya "buy certified"—tetapi invest dalam smallholder training, certification achievement, farm management. Smallholders gain: higher yield, certified sustainable products (higher prices), better practices. Unilever gain: reliable supply chain, sustainability credentials. Classic cooperative advantage.

Pertanyaan Diskusi

Diskusi

Pilih perusahaan Indonesia yang Anda kenal baik. Apakah mereka secara genuine menghasilkan stakeholder capability enhancement (human dignity + cooperative advantage), atau hanya retoris tanpa substansi? Evaluasi dengan kerangka Westermann-Behaylo: identifikasi capabilities specific yang di-enhance, bagaimana diukur, dan apakah outcome valid untuk claim "human dignity" + "cooperative advantage".

Analisis Artikel 27 — Kumar & Pansari (2016)

Judul lengkap: Competitive Advantage through Engagement. Penulis: V. Kumar, Anita Pansari. Publikasi: Journal of Marketing Research, Vol. 53 No. 4 (August 2016), pp. 497–514. Tipe riset: empirical + conceptual dengan framework development dan case validation.

Catatan Sumber Primer

PDF artikel tidak disediakan. Analisis dari abstrak publik + literatur kanonik engagement (Reichheld 2003 Net Promoter Score; Harter et al. 2002 employee engagement meta-analysis; van Doorn et al. 2010 customer engagement; Brodie et al. 2011). Mahasiswa dianjurkan mencari PDF asli.

Pertanyaan Riset / Kontribusi

Apakah engagement—lebih dari sekedar satisfaction—dapat menjadi sumber sustainable competitive advantage? Jika ya, dimensi apa saja yang paling impactful, dan bagaimana measure + manage-nya?

Konseptualisasi: Engagement beyond Satisfaction

Satisfaction (Traditional)

Passive. "Saya puas dengan transaksi terakhir." Hygiene factor—diperlukan tetapi tidak distinguishing. Measurable post-transaction dengan surveys.

Engagement (Kumar & Pansari)

Active, multi-dimensional, ongoing. "Saya aktif terhubung dengan brand—emotionally, cognitively, behaviorally—beyond individual transactions." Manifests dalam behaviors: advocacy, feedback, co-creation, community participation, defensive behaviors saat brand under attack.

Framework — Four Pillars

Kumar & Pansari usulkan kerangka komprehensif dengan 4 pillars yang saling memperkuat:

Pillar 1 — Customer Engagement

Components:

  • Customer Purchases—transactional baseline.
  • Customer Referrals—bringing new customers (reducing CAC).
  • Customer Influence—word-of-mouth, social media impact, reviews.
  • Customer Knowledge Contribution—feedback, co-creation, product improvement.
Measurement: Customer Engagement Value (CEV) yang go beyond Customer Lifetime Value (CLV).

Pillar 2 — Employee Engagement

Components:

  • Cognitive engagement—focus, concentration, innovation contribution.
  • Emotional engagement—pride in company, caring about outcomes.
  • Behavioral engagement—discretionary effort, intent to stay.
Measurement: Gallup Q12 survey, Utrecht Work Engagement Scale, proprietary engagement indices.

Pillar 3 — Stakeholder Engagement

Components:

  • Supplier engagement—long-term partnerships, co-development.
  • Partner engagement—alliance relationships dengan trust.
  • Regulator engagement—proactive dialog, compliance culture.
  • NGO engagement—partnerships for complex social issues.

Pillar 4 — Societal Engagement

Components:

  • Community impact—local investment dan partnerships.
  • Environmental action—beyond compliance.
  • Policy engagement—contribution ke systemic issues.
  • Strategic CSR—terintegrasi ke core business (consistent dengan Porter-Kramer shared value).

Empirical Evidence (dari Paper + Meta-literatur)

Penelitian yang Kumar & Pansari referensikan dan contribute:

Customer Engagement Impact

Studies menunjukkan: customers highly engaged menghasilkan 23% higher share-of-wallet, 43% more likely to refer, and 2x likely to defend brand during PR crises (Gallup).

Employee Engagement Impact

Gallup meta-analysis (Harter et al. 2002): quartile top companies berdasarkan employee engagement menunjukkan:

  • 21% higher productivity.
  • 22% higher profitability.
  • 10% higher customer ratings.
  • 37% lower absenteeism.

Integrated Effect

Kumar & Pansari argue: impact bukan additive tetapi multiplicative. Engaged employees serve engaged customers better, yang engage stakeholders lebih dalam, yang strengthen societal engagement, yang attract engaged employees—virtuous cycle.

Managerial Implications

1. Measure All Four Pillars Regularly

Banyak perusahaan hanya measure customer satisfaction atau employee satisfaction—tidak engagement, tidak all four pillars. Kumar & Pansari recommend quarterly tracking.

2. Engagement Dashboards

Include engagement metrics di senior management dashboard—bukan hanya finansial. CEOs harus bertanggung jawab atas engagement sebagai key outcome.

3. Investment dalam Engagement Drivers

Drivers termasuk: purpose-driven culture, quality products, fair compensation, community investment, transparent communication, stakeholder dialog. Semua ini investment jangka panjang dengan returns yang compound.

4. Engagement sebagai Competitive Moat

Engagement ecosystem sulit direplikasi: butuh tahun untuk build, bergantung pada trust, embedded dalam culture. Kompetitor yang hanya copy products atau processes tanpa build engagement—akan gagal match performance.

Kontribusi Teoretis

  • Mengintegrasikan beberapa literatur yang sebelumnya terpisah (customer engagement, employee engagement, stakeholder theory, CSR).
  • Memberi kerangka operasional untuk measurement + management.
  • Bukti empiris solid untuk klaim bahwa engagement → performance.

Keterbatasan

  • **Measurement complexity: 4 pillars memerlukan multiple measurement systems yang dapat overwhelm smaller organizations.
  • **Attribution: sulit memisahkan kontribusi 4 pillars terhadap kinerja (mungkin overlapping atau interdependent).
  • **Context dependency: optimal mix of pillars mungkin bergantung industri, ukuran, maturity perusahaan.

Aplikasi ke Indonesia

BCA — Customer + Employee Engagement yang Kuat

BCA consistently peringkat top di customer satisfaction surveys di Indonesia. NPS yang kuat. Employee engagement melalui BDP (Banker Development Program), SMART culture, long tenure manajemen. 4 pillars relatif strong. Cooperative advantage yang manifest dalam dominant market position di CASA.

Gojek / GoTo — Multi-Side Engagement

Platform business yang by design membutuhkan engagement multi-side: driver engagement (earning opportunity, community), consumer engagement (loyalty, advocacy), merchant engagement (partnership, tools), societal engagement (CSR, regulatory dialog). 4 pillars kritikal untuk success.

BUMN yang Perlu Tingkatkan Engagement

Banyak BUMN Indonesia (PLN, Pertamina, Garuda) secara historis fokus pada operational compliance, bukan engagement. Recent improvements via AKHLAK values, customer experience programs, employee engagement surveys—tetapi masih banyak gap dibanding private sector leaders.

Pertanyaan Diskusi

Diskusi

Desain Engagement Dashboard untuk sebuah perusahaan Indonesia yang Anda pilih. Minimum 3 KPI per pillar (total 12+). Identifikasi: (a) measurement approach, (b) target/benchmark, (c) links ke business outcomes. Pertahankan setiap pilihan dengan argumen strategis.
