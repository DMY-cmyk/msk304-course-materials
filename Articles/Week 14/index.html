Analisis Artikel 28 — Chen, Lin & Michel (2010)

Judul lengkap: Navigating in a Hypercompetitive Environment: The Roles of Action Aggressiveness and TMT Integration. Penulis: Ming-Jer Chen, Hsiu-Ching Lin, John G. Michel. Publikasi: Strategic Management Journal, Vol. 31 No. 13 (December 2010), pp. 1410–1430. Tipe riset: empirical quantitative—longitudinal analysis dengan data perusahaan publicly traded.

Catatan Sumber Primer

PDF tidak disediakan. Analisis dari abstrak publik + literatur kanonik competitive dynamics + TMT (D'Aveni 1994; Chen & Miller 1994; Hambrick et al. 1996; Ferrier 2001). Mahasiswa dianjurkan mencari PDF asli untuk pendalaman metodologis.

Pertanyaan Riset

Di environments yang hypercompetitive (D'Aveni 1994)—di mana competitive advantages temporary dan competitive interactions aggressive—apa yang menentukan firm performance? Lebih specifically, bagaimana action aggressiveness (frequency + intensity competitive moves) dan TMT integration (integrity fungsi top management team) berinteraksi untuk mempengaruhi kinerja?

Kerangka Teoretis

Chen, Lin & Michel mengintegrasikan:

  • Hypercompetition theory (D'Aveni 1994): competitive advantages temporary, aggressive action critical.
  • Competitive dynamics (Chen & Miller 1994; Ferrier 2001): firms yang take more actions, faster, perform better.
  • Upper echelons theory (Hambrick & Mason 1984): TMT characteristics shape strategic actions.
  • Behavioral theory of the firm (Cyert & March 1963): decision-making processes matter.

Hipotesis

H1 — Action Aggressiveness Positive Impact

Di hypercompetitive environments, firms dengan action aggressiveness lebih tinggi (more frequent, more intense competitive moves) achieve better performance.

H2 — TMT Integration Positive Impact

TMT integration (shared vision, communication, coordination, collaboration) positively impacts firm performance.

H3 — Moderation (Interaksi)

TMT integration memoderasi hubungan action aggressiveness → performance. Dengan TMT integration tinggi, action aggressiveness lebih effectively ditranslate menjadi performance. Dengan TMT fragmented, aggressive actions menjadi counterproductive.

Metode

Sample

Publicly traded firms di industri yang classified sebagai hypercompetitive (tech, computer, electronics). Longitudinal data—multiple years untuk capture dinamika.

Measurement

  • Action aggressiveness: content analysis dari public announcements, press releases—count of competitive actions (pricing, new products, marketing, M&A) and their intensity.
  • TMT integration: survey measures + archival data on TMT composition, tenure, stability, communication patterns.
  • Performance: financial metrics (ROA, market share growth, stock performance).
  • Controls: firm size, age, industry, prior performance.

Analysis

Hierarchical regression untuk test main effects dan interaction. Longitudinal analysis untuk temporal dynamics.

Temuan Utama

Finding 1 — H1 Supported

Action aggressiveness positive associated dengan performance di hypercompetitive environments. Firms yang passive mengalami decline; firms yang aggressive (price moves, product launches, M&A, marketing campaigns) outperform.

Finding 2 — H2 Partially Supported

TMT integration has positive main effect—firms dengan integrated TMT outperform fragmented TMT. Effect size moderate tetapi statistically significant.

Finding 3 — H3 Strongly Supported

Interaction effect significant: action aggressiveness × TMT integration. Firms dengan HIGH action aggressiveness + HIGH TMT integration = highest performance. Firms dengan HIGH action aggressiveness + LOW TMT integration = worst performance. TMT integration is crucial moderator—tanpa integration, aggressive actions menjadi destructive.

Finding 4 — Implication for Passive Firms

Firms yang passive (low action aggressiveness) perform worse than aggressive firms regardless of TMT integration. Dalam hypercompetition, passivity = slow death.

Kontribusi Teoretis

1. Integrasi Competitive Dynamics + TMT Research

Literatur competitive dynamics traditionally fokus pada firm-level actions. Literatur TMT fokus pada management characteristics. Chen et al. menunjukkan bahwa keduanya interact—action aggressiveness effectiveness bergantung pada TMT capability untuk coordinate dan execute.

2. Nuance untuk "Aggressive Action" Prescription

Literatur hypercompetition (D'Aveni, Ferrier) cenderung merekomendasikan aggressive action universally. Chen et al. memberi nuance: aggressive action harus didukung TMT integration. Otherwise, prescription becomes counterproductive.

3. Empirical Validation for D'Aveni

Memberi bukti empiris quantitative untuk klaim D'Aveni tentang pentingnya aggressive competitive maneuvering di hypercompetitive environments.

Keterbatasan

  • US-centric sample: findings mungkin berbeda di emerging markets dengan institutional voids, family-dominated firms, atau state-owned enterprises.
  • Focus pada financial performance: tidak mengukur non-financial outcomes (stakeholder engagement, innovation capability).
  • Measurement challenges untuk TMT integration: relying on survey + archival proxies—subjective elements.
  • Temporal scope: findings dari period spesifik yang might not generalize ke different competitive landscapes.

Aplikasi ke Indonesia

Ride-Hailing Triopoly: Testing Chen-Lin-Michel Framework

GoTo, Grab, ShopeeFood—semua aggressive dalam competitive actions (pricing, promotions, new features, strategic alliances). Success differences likely correlate dengan TMT integration quality:

  • GoTo post-merger facing TMT integration challenge (Gojek + Tokopedia cultures).
  • Grab dengan relatively stable senior leadership (Anthony Tan sebagai CEO since founding).
  • ShopeeFood sebagai part of Sea Group dengan clear leadership chain.
Empirical prediction: firms dengan better TMT integration akan outperform pada medium-term basis, meskipun semua menjalankan aggressive actions.

E-Commerce Shakeout

Tokopedia, Shopee, Lazada, Bukalapak—semua hypercompetitive environment. Bukalapak post-shakeout struggle likely correlate dengan combination of reduced aggressiveness (slow pivot) + TMT challenges (management changes, investor pressure). Tokopedia-TikTok merger tested TMT integration lintas-organizations.

Banking Digital Transformation

Perbankan Indonesia dalam transition phase—industries transitioning to more hypercompetitive. BCA Jahja Setiaatmadja represents HIGH TMT integration. Bank Mandiri post-merger complex TMT. Bank Jago agile TMT with startup culture. Empirical test: which leadership+action combination outperform over next 5-10 years?

Pertanyaan Diskusi

Diskusi

Pilih satu industri hypercompetitive Indonesia. Identifikasi 3-4 major players. Estimasi masing-masing pada dua dimensi: (a) action aggressiveness (berapa aggressive competitive moves?), (b) TMT integration (berapa integrated leadership?). Predict relative performance berdasarkan Chen-Lin-Michel framework. Validate dengan bukti public yang available.

Analisis Artikel 29 — Jansen, Vera & Crossan (2009)

Judul lengkap: Strategic Leadership for Exploration and Exploitation: The Moderating Role of Environmental Dynamism. Penulis: Justin J. P. Jansen, Dusya Vera, Mary Crossan. Publikasi: The Leadership Quarterly, Vol. 20 No. 1 (February 2009), pp. 5–18. Tipe riset: empirical quantitative—survey-based dengan hierarchical linear modeling.

Catatan Sumber Primer

PDF tidak disediakan. Analisis dari abstrak publik + literatur kanonik leadership + ambidexterity (Bass 1985; March 1991; Tushman & O'Reilly 1996; Jansen et al. 2006). Mahasiswa dianjurkan mencari PDF asli.

Pertanyaan Riset

Bagaimana different leadership styles (transformational vs. transactional) mempengaruhi organizational exploration vs. exploitation? Apakah dampak leadership style dimoderasi oleh environmental dynamism?

Kerangka Teoretis

Integrasi tiga literatur:

1. Leadership Theory (Bass 1985)

Transformational leadership: vision, inspirational motivation, intellectual stimulation, individualized consideration.

Transactional leadership: contingent rewards, management-by-exception (active and passive).

2. Exploration-Exploitation (March 1991)

Exploration: search for new possibilities, variation, experimentation.
Exploitation: refinement, efficiency, selection of known alternatives.

3. Environmental Dynamism

Degree of change dan instability di industry environment—rate of product/service innovation, technology change, customer preference shifts, competitor actions.

Hipotesis

H1 — Transformational → Exploration

Transformational leadership positively related to exploration. Vision + inspiration + intellectual stimulation foster novel thinking and experimentation.

H2 — Transactional → Exploitation

Transactional leadership positively related to exploitation. Clear goals + contingent rewards foster efficiency and execution of existing strategies.

H3a — Dynamism Moderates Transformational-Exploration

Relationship between transformational leadership dan exploration stronger di high dynamism (change-intensive) environments. Under dynamism, exploration is more critical for survival, and transformational leadership capitalizes this.

H3b — Dynamism Moderates Transactional-Exploitation

Relationship between transactional leadership dan exploitation stronger di low dynamism (stable) environments. Under stability, exploitation is sufficient, and transactional leadership optimally fits.

Metode

Sample

Survey sample dari organizations di various industries. Multi-source data—CEOs rate own leadership, subordinates rate exploration/exploitation activities, independent measures of environmental dynamism.

Measurement

  • Leadership styles: Multifactor Leadership Questionnaire (MLQ) subscales.
  • Exploration & Exploitation: Jansen et al. (2006) scales.
  • Environmental dynamism: Dess & Beard (1984) scales—rate of change in technology, competitors, customer preferences.

Analysis

Hierarchical linear regression to test main effects dan interaction effects. Controlled untuk firm size, age, industry.

Temuan Utama

Finding 1 — H1 Supported

Transformational leadership significantly positively related to exploration. Leaders yang articulate vision + stimulate intellectually encourage organization untuk explore new possibilities.

Finding 2 — H2 Supported

Transactional leadership significantly positively related to exploitation. Leaders yang set clear goals + provide contingent rewards foster execution dari existing strategies.

Finding 3 — H3a Supported

Dynamism strengthens transformational leadership → exploration relationship. Under high change, transformational leadership truly valuable untuk driving exploration.

Finding 4 — H3b Supported

Dynamism weakens transactional leadership → exploitation relationship (i.e., dynamism strengthens transformational need). In high-dynamism environments, transactional leadership alone insufficient—organization needs exploration alongside exploitation.

Finding 5 — Ambidextrous Need in Dynamic Environments

Di dynamic environments, organizations ideally combine transformational (for exploration) and transactional (for exploitation) leadership. Single-style leadership insufficient.

Kontribusi Teoretis

1. Linkages Leadership-Ambidexterity

Previous ambidexterity research focused pada organizational structure (Tushman & O'Reilly 1996). Jansen et al. introduce leadership dimension—how leaders enable or inhibit ambidexterity.

2. Contextual Leadership Theory

Challenges "one best leadership style" narratives. Different styles optimal di different contexts. Validates contingency perspective.

3. Practical Implications

CEO development, TMT composition, succession planning—harus consider environmental dynamism dan balance antara transformational + transactional styles.

Keterbatasan

  • Cross-sectional data: dapat suggest correlation tetapi kausalitas tidak fully established.
  • Survey-based: leadership styles self-reported oleh CEOs—potential social desirability bias.
  • Sample concentration: Western firms (Netherlands + North America)—generalizability ke emerging markets uncertain.
  • Simplification: transformational-transactional dichotomy mungkin oversimplify leadership complexity.

Aplikasi ke Indonesia

Case 1 — Nadiem Makarim Era di Gojek (2010-2019)

Environmental dynamism: sangat tinggi—emerging ride-hailing industry, rapid technology change, shifting regulasi.
Leadership style: predominantly transformational—articulating vision "empower Indonesia through technology", intellectual stimulation untuk engineer team.
Exploration: tinggi—continuous new verticals (GoFood, GoSend, GoPay, etc.).
Fit: strong fit dengan Jansen framework—transformational × high dynamism → strong exploration. Hasil: rapid growth, category creation.

Case 2 — Jahja Setiaatmadja di BCA

Environmental dynamism: moderate (perbankan Indonesia—stable regulatory but increasing digital disruption).
Leadership style: mix transformational + transactional. Transformational dimensions: SMART culture building, digital transformation vision. Transactional: clear performance metrics, disciplined execution.
Exploration + Exploitation: balanced. Mobile banking development (exploration) + branch efficiency optimization (exploitation).
Fit: consistent dengan ambidextrous leadership hypothesis. Moderate dynamism rewards balanced approach.

Case 3 — BUMN Legacy Leadership

Banyak BUMN Indonesia secara historis lebih transactional—operational focus, execution discipline, less vision articulation. Consistent dengan less dynamic environments (regulated utilities, state-mandated roles). Tetapi ketika environment berubah (energy transition, digital economy), BUMN yang gagal shift menuju transformational leadership mengalami decline relative ke private sector competitors.

Case 4 — Family Conglomerate Transitions

Second/third generation transitions di konglomerat Indonesia (Astra, Salim, Djarum, Sinar Mas) adalah moment of leadership test. Founder-generation cenderung transformational (building conglomerate). Second generation sering transactional (professionalizing operations). Third generation challenges: reconfigure untuk digital era—need transformational leadership again.

Pertanyaan Diskusi

Diskusi

Pilih satu perusahaan Indonesia. Assess:

  • Environmental dynamism yang dihadapi (low/medium/high).
  • Current leadership style (predominantly transformational / transactional / mixed).
  • Current balance exploration-exploitation.
  • Apakah leadership style fit dengan environment? Jika tidak, apa rekomendasi?
Gunakan Jansen et al. framework untuk sistematis analysis.
