## Sidebar 1 — Jahja Setiaatmadja di BCA: Long-Tenure Strategic Leadership

**Jangkar konsep:** contextual leadership—balanced transformational-transactional style dalam moderate-dynamism environment; TMT integration yang dibangun via continuity + internal culture; contrast to high-turnover leadership.

Jahja Setiaatmadja sebagai Presdir BCA <strong>sejak 2011</strong>—lebih dari 13 tahun continuity. Ini anomaly dalam Indonesian banking industri yang sering mengalami CEO turnover. Strategic implications dari continuity ini warrant careful analysis.

### Environmental Dynamism Context

Perbankan konsumer Indonesia dari Jansen et al. perspective:
- <strong>Historis (pre-2015):</strong> relatively low dynamism. Regulated industry, clear competitors (Big-4 banks), stable consumer behavior.
- <strong>Recent (2015+):</strong> increasing dynamism. Digital banks emerging (Bank Jago, SeaBank, Allo), fintech challengers, changing consumer preferences, QRIS standardization.

BCA berada di industry yang <em>transitioning from stable toward more dynamic</em>.

### Jahja's Leadership Style Analysis

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>Transformational Elements</h4>
<ul>
<li><strong>Vision articulation:</strong> "bank pilihan utama andalan masyarakat" consistent dan diperkuat.</li>
<li><strong>Intellectual stimulation:</strong> encouraging digital transformation, fostering BDP talent untuk innovative thinking.</li>
<li><strong>Idealized influence:</strong> respected public figure, role model untuk banking professional integrity.</li>
<li><strong>Individualized consideration:</strong> known untuk personal connection dengan senior executives.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>Transactional Elements</h4>
<ul>
<li><strong>Clear performance metrics:</strong> CASA ratio, NPL, ROE tracked rigorously.</li>
<li><strong>Contingent rewards:</strong> BCA known untuk performance-based compensation.</li>
<li><strong>Management-by-exception:</strong> intervention saat KPI tidak tercapai.</li>
<li><strong>Performance monitoring:</strong> robust regular reviews.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>Balanced / Ambidextrous Leadership</h4>
<p>Jahja exemplifies balanced style—not purely one or the other. Consistent dengan Jansen et al. hypothesis: moderate dynamism rewards balanced leadership. Dalam high dynamism, pure transformational needed; dalam pure stability, pure transactional sufficient; dalam between, balance wins.</p>
</div>

### TMT Integration Consequences

Long-tenure CEO + culture of internal promotion = extremely integrated TMT:
- TMT members grew up together, trust established over decades.
- Shared mental models (same training, same culture).
- Predictable decision processes.
- Lower politics karena tenure stability.

<strong>Consequence:</strong> decision-making cepat, execution coherent, aggressive actions effectively coordinated when taken. Contoh: digital transformation BCA (BCA Mobile, Welma, OneKlik, QRIS integration) berjalan cepat dan coherent.

### Debate — Continuity vs. Fresh Perspective

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>Pro-Continuity Arguments</h4>
<ul>
<li>Deep institutional knowledge.</li>
<li>Established relationships.</li>
<li>Predictable strategic direction.</li>
<li>TMT integration benefits.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>Concerns about Continuity</h4>
<ul>
<li>Risk of strategic persistence (upper echelons theory—long tenure = commit to status quo).</li>
<li>Fresh perspective limited.</li>
<li>Succession risk—what happens post-Jahja?</li>
<li>Adaptation capability if environment becomes much more dynamic.</li>
</ul>
</div>

BCA's approach: continuity with pipeline development. BDP (Banker Development Program) continuously grooming next generation leaders. This is <em>conscious succession management</em>, bukan just accident.

## Sidebar 2 — Telkom TMT: BUMN Ambidextrous Challenge

**Jangkar konsep:** BUMN legacy TMT menghadapi transformasi digital; contextual ambidexterity requirement; exploration (5G, cloud, enterprise digital) + exploitation (legacy connectivity, fixed broadband); governance complexity.

Telkom Indonesia (IDX: TLKM) adalah <strong>case interesting untuk analisis ambidextrous leadership di konteks BUMN</strong>. Perusahaan memiliki:
- Legacy business yang matang (fixed line, broadband)—need exploitation.
- Growth segments (enterprise digital, cloud, data center, 5G mobile)—need exploration.
- Ownership pemerintah dengan policy mandates—adding complexity to leadership dynamics.

### Environmental Dynamism

Telekomunikasi Indonesia:
- <strong>Mature segments (mobile voice, SMS):</strong> low dynamism, declining demand.
- <strong>Evolving segments (data, mobile internet):</strong> moderate dynamism.
- <strong>Emerging segments (5G, enterprise cloud, IoT, AI services):</strong> high dynamism.

Telkom operates across all three simultaneously—maximal dynamism variance.

### TMT Structure dan Ambidextrous Challenges

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>BUMN Governance Layer</h4>
<p>Dewan Komisaris appointed via government process. Dewan Direksi mix of professional + government-influenced appointments. This layered structure dapat create TMT fragmentation antara professional management objectives (return on investment) dan government mandates (national development, employment, connectivity targets).</p>
</div>

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>CEO Tenure Typically Shorter</h4>
<p>BUMN CEOs appointed 5-year terms, sometimes renewed. Compared to Jahja at BCA (13+ years), BUMN leadership continuity lower. Implication: TMT integration harder to build, strategic direction shifts dengan administrasi changes.</p>
</div>

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>Ambidextrous Requirements</h4>
<p>Telkom requires:
<ul>
<li><strong>Exploitation leadership</strong> untuk legacy operations (transactional—clear KPIs, operational excellence, cost management).</li>
<li><strong>Exploration leadership</strong> untuk digital growth (transformational—vision untuk digital future, intellectual stimulation, risk tolerance).</li>
</ul>
Challenge: finding leaders yang dapat do both, atau TMT yang complementary.</p>
</div>

### Strategic Responses — Structural Ambidexterity

Telkom's approach: <em>structural separation</em>:
- <strong>Core legacy operations</strong> under Telkom main company—transactional leadership.
- <strong>Digital subsidiaries</strong> (Telkomsel—mobile, Metranet—enterprise, Telkomsat—satellite, Indico—digital platforms, TelkomSigma—IT)—each with dedicated leadership focused on their domain.
- <strong>Mitratel</strong> (menara)—separated, IPO'd untuk financial independence.

This creates multiple TMT layers—complex tetapi dapat enable ambidexterity.

### Leadership Challenges

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>1. Speed of Decisions</h4>
<p>BUMN governance + multiple subsidiaries = slow decision-making. Di industri dengan dynamism meningkat, speed matters. Telkom's response: delegated authorities, empowered subsidiary leadership.</p>
</div>

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>2. Talent Competition</h4>
<p>Digital tech talent preferred private sector (GoTo, Bank Jago, startups). BUMN compensation structures kurang kompetitif. Telkom's response: subsidiary-level compensation flexibility, partnerships (Telkomsel with Singtel, etc.).</p>
</div>

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>3. Political-Commercial Balance</h4>
<p>BUMN harus balance national development mandates dengan commercial returns. This inherently creates TMT tensions between different objective functions. Integration requires skilled leadership yang dapat articulate how these can align.</p>
</div>

### Kontras Tiga Flagship Minggu 14

| Contoh | Environment | Leadership Style | TMT Integration |
|--------|-------------|-----------------|-----------------|
| GoTo-Grab-ShopeeFood | Hypercompetitive | Variable (founder vs. professional) | Variable—TMT integration differentiator |
| Jahja di BCA | Moderate dynamism | Balanced (transformational + transactional) | Extremely high (long tenure + internal promotion) |
| Telkom TMT | Mixed (hi/low dynamism) | Needs ambidextrous | Challenged by BUMN structure complexity |

Ketiga menunjukkan spectrum:
- <strong>Hypercompetition</strong>: demands integrated aggressive TMT.
- <strong>Moderate dynamism</strong>: rewards balanced leadership + continuity.
- <strong>Legacy + emerging mix</strong>: requires structural ambidexterity.

### Pelajaran Strategic Leadership Capstone

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>1. Leadership Style Must Match Environment</h4>
<p>Jansen et al. dan Chen-Lin-Michel converge: context matters. No "one best leadership". Leaders harus assess environment dan calibrate style.</p>
</div>

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>2. TMT Integration adalah Multiplier</h4>
<p>Good leadership amplified oleh integrated TMT; weakened oleh fragmentation. Building TMT integration adalah long-term investment.</p>
</div>

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>3. Succession Matters</h4>
<p>Long-tenure continuity (Jahja BCA) benefits present but creates succession risk. Short tenure (some BUMN) creates integration challenges. Intentional succession planning crucial.</p>
</div>

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>4. Organizational Structure Supports Leadership</h4>
<p>Structural ambidexterity (Telkom separated digital subsidiaries) enables different leadership styles where appropriate. Organization design is leadership design.</p>
</div>

### Final Capstone Reflection

Minggu 14 menutup mata kuliah dengan dua tema integratif. Hypercompetition forces us to reconsider classical strategy assumptions. Strategic leadership determines whether organization can actually translate strategy into outcomes. Together, they remind us: <strong>strategic management is ultimately about people making complex choices under uncertainty—frameworks help, but judgment, integration, and leadership execution determine success.</strong>
