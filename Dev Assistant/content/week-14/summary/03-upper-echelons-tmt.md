## Upper Echelons Theory (Hambrick &amp; Mason 1984)

Donald Hambrick &amp; Phyllis Mason dalam artikel seminal <em>Academy of Management Review</em> (1984) mengusulkan <strong>upper echelons theory</strong>: <em>organizations are a reflection of their top managers</em>.

### Inti Argumen

Strategic decisions di organisasi kompleks sering complicated—involving ambiguity, uncertainty, multiple interpretations. Dalam kondisi ini, <em>cognitive base</em> dari decision-makers (top management team) menjadi critical. Manager membawa:

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>Demographic Characteristics</h4>
<ul>
<li><strong>Age:</strong> older managers cenderung risk-averse.</li>
<li><strong>Tenure:</strong> longer tenure cenderung commit ke status quo.</li>
<li><strong>Functional background:</strong> finance-heavy TMT cenderung defensive, marketing-heavy cenderung expansive.</li>
<li><strong>Education:</strong> formal education levels correlate dengan strategic choices.</li>
<li><strong>Socioeconomic background:</strong> affect risk tolerance, stakeholder orientation.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>Psychological Characteristics</h4>
<ul>
<li><strong>Cognitive base:</strong> mental models dari past experiences.</li>
<li><strong>Values:</strong> what managers consider important (profit, growth, social impact).</li>
<li><strong>Risk tolerance:</strong> willingness to take bold actions.</li>
</ul>
</div>

### Implikasi

<strong>Organizational outcomes</strong>—strategy choices, innovation rate, financial performance, response to crisis—reflect TMT characteristics. Untuk understand organization, study its TMT.

### Research Evidence

Decades of research menguji upper echelons:
- TMT functional diversity → strategic diversity dan innovation.
- TMT tenure homogeneity → strategic persistence (resistance to change).
- TMT age heterogeneity → better adaptation to dynamic environments.
- TMT education → strategic sophistication + scanning behavior.

### TMT Integration (Chen, Lin &amp; Michel 2010)

Selain <em>characteristics</em>, TMT <strong>integration</strong>—bagaimana TMT berfungsi sebagai unit—matters significantly.

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>Dimensions of TMT Integration</h4>
<ul>
<li><strong>Shared vision:</strong> alignment pada strategic direction.</li>
<li><strong>Communication quality:</strong> open dialogue, information sharing.</li>
<li><strong>Decision-making coordination:</strong> integrated decisions, not siloed.</li>
<li><strong>Collaborative behavior:</strong> mutual support, constructive conflict.</li>
<li><strong>Role clarity:</strong> understanding each other's responsibilities.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>Why TMT Integration Matters in Hypercompetition</h4>
<p>Di hypercompetitive environments yang memerlukan aggressive action:
<ul>
<li><strong>Speed:</strong> integrated TMT dapat decide + execute cepat.</li>
<li><strong>Coherence:</strong> aggressive actions consistent, tidak contradict.</li>
<li><strong>Resilience:</strong> ketika actions fail (inevitable in hypercompetition), integrated TMT recover together.</li>
<li><strong>Learning:</strong> integrated TMT extract lessons dari outcomes, improve future decisions.</li>
</ul>
TMT yang fragmented menghasilkan:
<ul>
<li>Slow decisions (political conflicts).</li>
<li>Contradictory signals to organization.</li>
<li>Execution failures (different TMT members pulling different directions).</li>
<li>Blame culture when actions fail.</li>
</ul></p>
</div>

### Aplikasi ke Indonesia

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>BCA — High TMT Integration</h4>
<p>Jahja Setiaatmadja sebagai Presdir sejak 2011—kontinuitas kepemimpinan yang langka. TMT BCA consistently integrated via shared SMART values, internal promotion culture (executives grew together), predictable decision processes. Hasil: execution yang konsisten dan strong performance selama 2 decades.</p>
</div>

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>Garuda Indonesia — Low TMT Integration (Historis)</h4>
<p>Pre-2020 Garuda: multiple CEO changes, politik yang kompleks (BUMN dengan pressure politis), conflicting priorities. TMT fragmented antara commercial vs. government mandate perspectives. Contributing factor untuk crisis yang accumulated.</p>
</div>

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>GoTo — TMT Integration Challenge Post-Merger</h4>
<p>Merger Gojek + Tokopedia (2021) menciptakan TMT integration challenge—different corporate cultures (Gojek as technical ops, Tokopedia as marketplace), different decision processes, different leadership styles. Post-merger integration journey masih berjalan. Kinerja post-pivot yang solid menunjukkan TMT integration sedang membaik.</p>
</div>

### Implikasi untuk Strategic Management

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>1. Hire, Develop, Manage TMT Carefully</h4>
<p>TMT composition = strategic decision. Siapa yang ada di TMT (background, values, styles) akan shape strategic behavior organization for years to come.</p>
</div>

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>2. Build TMT Integration Deliberately</h4>
<p>Integration tidak spontan. Invest dalam: TMT retreats, communication norms, shared planning processes, conflict resolution mechanisms.</p>
</div>

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>3. Diversity Plus Integration</h4>
<p>TMT yang homogeneous dan integrated dapat groupthink. TMT yang diverse tetapi fragmented chaos. Sweet spot: diverse perspectives plus integrated functioning. Hardest untuk achieve—membutuhkan intentional leadership.</p>
</div>

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>4. Succession Planning as Strategic Priority</h4>
<p>TMT transitions adalah moments of vulnerability. Good strategic management include rigorous succession planning untuk maintain integration across leadership changes.</p>
</div>
