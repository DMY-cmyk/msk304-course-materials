## Ikhtisar Minggu 14 (Capstone)

Referensi: **Henry Ch.3** *(External Environment: Hypercompetition)* · **Henry Ch.11** *(Strategic Leadership &amp; Change)* · **Art.28 Chen, Lin &amp; Michel (2010)** · **Art.29 Jansen, Vera &amp; Crossan (2009)**

Minggu 14 adalah <strong>capstone</strong> dari mata kuliah Manajemen Strategik Kontemporer. Dua topik yang dipilih untuk minggu final dengan sengaja:

<div class="pull-quote">
In hypercompetitive environments, the rules of strategy change: sustainable competitive advantage becomes elusive, and the capability to continuously create and destroy advantages—led by integrated top management teams—becomes the source of superior performance.
<cite>Adaptasi dari Chen, Lin &amp; Michel — Organization Science (2010)</cite>
</div>

### Tema 1 — Hypercompetition

Richard D'Aveni (1994) dalam <em>Hypercompetition</em> menantang premise tradisional strategi: <em>sustainable competitive advantage</em>. Di industri modern yang paling dinamis, advantages adalah <strong>temporary</strong>—competitor respond cepat, technology bergeser, consumer preferences berubah. Perusahaan yang berhasil di hypercompetition bukan yang build <em>unassailable fortresses</em> tetapi yang continuously create <em>sequences of temporary advantages</em>.

Chen, Lin &amp; Michel (2010) memperdalam dengan studi empiris: <strong>action aggressiveness</strong> (frequency + intensity competitive moves) meningkatkan kinerja di hypercompetitive environments, tetapi hanya jika didukung <strong>TMT integration</strong> yang strong. Tanpa TMT integration, aggressive actions become uncoordinated dan destructive.

### Tema 2 — Strategic Leadership

<em>Upper echelons theory</em> (Hambrick &amp; Mason 1984): organizational outcomes reflect TMT characteristics. Strategic leadership bukan hanya soft skill—ia <strong>fundamental driver</strong> of organizational behavior dan performance.

Jansen, Vera &amp; Crossan (2009) mengintegrasikan strategic leadership dengan tension exploration-exploitation (March 1991):
- <strong>Transformational leadership</strong> (vision, intellectual stimulation, inspirational motivation) mendukung <em>exploration</em>—innovation, experimentation, new capabilities.
- <strong>Transactional leadership</strong> (clear goals, contingent rewards, management-by-exception) mendukung <em>exploitation</em>—efficiency, execution, refinement of existing capabilities.

<strong>Ambidextrous leadership</strong>—kemampuan untuk enable keduanya—adalah capability puncak yang sangat sulit achieve tetapi critical di dynamic environments.

### Mengapa Capstone

Minggu 14 mengintegrasikan:
- <strong>Minggu 3</strong> (external environment): hypercompetition adalah extreme case.
- <strong>Minggu 4</strong> (VRIN + dynamic capabilities): temporary advantages menuntut dynamic capabilities.
- <strong>Minggu 5-7</strong> (strategy formulation): rapid strategy formulation di hypercompetition.
- <strong>Minggu 11</strong> (execution): TMT integration = prerequisite for effective execution.
- <strong>Minggu 12</strong> (business model innovation): mechanism untuk create new temporary advantages.
- <strong>Minggu 13</strong> (stakeholder engagement): moderator of hypercompetition impact.

Untuk mahasiswa Magister Akuntansi yang akan menjadi senior professionals atau executives—minggu ini adalah preparation untuk realitas strategic management di era digital: <em>continuous adaptation under uncertainty</em>.
