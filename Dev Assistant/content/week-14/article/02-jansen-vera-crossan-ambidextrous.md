## Analisis Artikel 29 — Jansen, Vera &amp; Crossan (2009)

**Judul lengkap:** *Strategic Leadership for Exploration and Exploitation: The Moderating Role of Environmental Dynamism.*
**Penulis:** Justin J. P. Jansen, Dusya Vera, Mary Crossan.
**Publikasi:** The Leadership Quarterly, Vol. 20 No. 1 (February 2009), pp. 5–18.
**Tipe riset:** empirical quantitative—survey-based dengan hierarchical linear modeling.

<div class="callout disclosure">
<div class="tt">Catatan Sumber Primer</div>
<p>PDF tidak disediakan. Analisis dari <strong>abstrak publik + literatur kanonik leadership + ambidexterity</strong> (Bass 1985; March 1991; Tushman &amp; O'Reilly 1996; Jansen et al. 2006). Mahasiswa dianjurkan mencari PDF asli.</p>
</div>

### Pertanyaan Riset

Bagaimana different leadership styles (<em>transformational</em> vs. <em>transactional</em>) mempengaruhi organizational exploration vs. exploitation? Apakah dampak leadership style dimoderasi oleh environmental dynamism?

### Kerangka Teoretis

Integrasi tiga literatur:

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>1. Leadership Theory (Bass 1985)</h4>
<p><strong>Transformational leadership:</strong> vision, inspirational motivation, intellectual stimulation, individualized consideration.<br><br>
<strong>Transactional leadership:</strong> contingent rewards, management-by-exception (active and passive).</p>
</div>

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>2. Exploration-Exploitation (March 1991)</h4>
<p><strong>Exploration:</strong> search for new possibilities, variation, experimentation.<br>
<strong>Exploitation:</strong> refinement, efficiency, selection of known alternatives.</p>
</div>

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>3. Environmental Dynamism</h4>
<p>Degree of change dan instability di industry environment—rate of product/service innovation, technology change, customer preference shifts, competitor actions.</p>
</div>

### Hipotesis

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>H1 — Transformational → Exploration</h4>
<p>Transformational leadership positively related to exploration. Vision + inspiration + intellectual stimulation foster novel thinking and experimentation.</p>
</div>

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>H2 — Transactional → Exploitation</h4>
<p>Transactional leadership positively related to exploitation. Clear goals + contingent rewards foster efficiency and execution of existing strategies.</p>
</div>

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>H3a — Dynamism Moderates Transformational-Exploration</h4>
<p>Relationship between transformational leadership dan exploration <em>stronger</em> di high dynamism (change-intensive) environments. Under dynamism, exploration is more critical for survival, and transformational leadership capitalizes this.</p>
</div>

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>H3b — Dynamism Moderates Transactional-Exploitation</h4>
<p>Relationship between transactional leadership dan exploitation <em>stronger</em> di low dynamism (stable) environments. Under stability, exploitation is sufficient, and transactional leadership optimally fits.</p>
</div>

### Metode

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>Sample</h4>
<p>Survey sample dari organizations di various industries. Multi-source data—CEOs rate own leadership, subordinates rate exploration/exploitation activities, independent measures of environmental dynamism.</p>
</div>

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>Measurement</h4>
<ul>
<li><strong>Leadership styles:</strong> Multifactor Leadership Questionnaire (MLQ) subscales.</li>
<li><strong>Exploration &amp; Exploitation:</strong> Jansen et al. (2006) scales.</li>
<li><strong>Environmental dynamism:</strong> Dess &amp; Beard (1984) scales—rate of change in technology, competitors, customer preferences.</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>Analysis</h4>
<p>Hierarchical linear regression to test main effects dan interaction effects. Controlled untuk firm size, age, industry.</p>
</div>

### Temuan Utama

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>Finding 1 — H1 Supported</h4>
<p>Transformational leadership significantly positively related to exploration. Leaders yang articulate vision + stimulate intellectually encourage organization untuk explore new possibilities.</p>
</div>

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>Finding 2 — H2 Supported</h4>
<p>Transactional leadership significantly positively related to exploitation. Leaders yang set clear goals + provide contingent rewards foster execution dari existing strategies.</p>
</div>

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>Finding 3 — H3a Supported</h4>
<p>Dynamism strengthens transformational leadership → exploration relationship. Under high change, transformational leadership truly valuable untuk driving exploration.</p>
</div>

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>Finding 4 — H3b Supported</h4>
<p>Dynamism weakens transactional leadership → exploitation relationship (i.e., dynamism strengthens transformational need). In high-dynamism environments, transactional leadership alone insufficient—organization needs exploration alongside exploitation.</p>
</div>

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>Finding 5 — Ambidextrous Need in Dynamic Environments</h4>
<p>Di dynamic environments, organizations ideally combine transformational (for exploration) and transactional (for exploitation) leadership. Single-style leadership insufficient.</p>
</div>

### Kontribusi Teoretis

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>1. Linkages Leadership-Ambidexterity</h4>
<p>Previous ambidexterity research focused pada organizational structure (Tushman &amp; O'Reilly 1996). Jansen et al. introduce leadership dimension—how leaders enable or inhibit ambidexterity.</p>
</div>

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>2. Contextual Leadership Theory</h4>
<p>Challenges "one best leadership style" narratives. Different styles optimal di different contexts. Validates contingency perspective.</p>
</div>

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>3. Practical Implications</h4>
<p>CEO development, TMT composition, succession planning—harus consider environmental dynamism dan balance antara transformational + transactional styles.</p>
</div>

### Keterbatasan

- <strong>Cross-sectional data:</strong> dapat suggest correlation tetapi kausalitas tidak fully established.
- <strong>Survey-based:</strong> leadership styles self-reported oleh CEOs—potential social desirability bias.
- <strong>Sample concentration:</strong> Western firms (Netherlands + North America)—generalizability ke emerging markets uncertain.
- <strong>Simplification:</strong> transformational-transactional dichotomy mungkin oversimplify leadership complexity.

### Aplikasi ke Indonesia

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>Case 1 — Nadiem Makarim Era di Gojek (2010-2019)</h4>
<p><strong>Environmental dynamism:</strong> sangat tinggi—emerging ride-hailing industry, rapid technology change, shifting regulasi.<br>
<strong>Leadership style:</strong> predominantly transformational—articulating vision "empower Indonesia through technology", intellectual stimulation untuk engineer team.<br>
<strong>Exploration:</strong> tinggi—continuous new verticals (GoFood, GoSend, GoPay, etc.).<br>
<strong>Fit:</strong> strong fit dengan Jansen framework—transformational × high dynamism → strong exploration. Hasil: rapid growth, category creation.</p>
</div>

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>Case 2 — Jahja Setiaatmadja di BCA</h4>
<p><strong>Environmental dynamism:</strong> moderate (perbankan Indonesia—stable regulatory but increasing digital disruption).<br>
<strong>Leadership style:</strong> mix transformational + transactional. Transformational dimensions: SMART culture building, digital transformation vision. Transactional: clear performance metrics, disciplined execution.<br>
<strong>Exploration + Exploitation:</strong> balanced. Mobile banking development (exploration) + branch efficiency optimization (exploitation).<br>
<strong>Fit:</strong> consistent dengan ambidextrous leadership hypothesis. Moderate dynamism rewards balanced approach.</p>
</div>

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>Case 3 — BUMN Legacy Leadership</h4>
<p>Banyak BUMN Indonesia secara historis lebih transactional—operational focus, execution discipline, less vision articulation. Consistent dengan <em>less dynamic environments</em> (regulated utilities, state-mandated roles). Tetapi ketika environment berubah (energy transition, digital economy), BUMN yang gagal shift menuju transformational leadership mengalami decline relative ke private sector competitors.</p>
</div>

<div class="concept-card">
<h4>Case 4 — Family Conglomerate Transitions</h4>
<p>Second/third generation transitions di konglomerat Indonesia (Astra, Salim, Djarum, Sinar Mas) adalah moment of leadership test. Founder-generation cenderung transformational (building conglomerate). Second generation sering transactional (professionalizing operations). Third generation challenges: reconfigure untuk digital era—need transformational leadership again.</p>
</div>

### Pertanyaan Diskusi

<div class="callout check">
<div class="tt">Diskusi</div>
<p>Pilih satu perusahaan Indonesia. Assess:
<ul>
<li>Environmental dynamism yang dihadapi (low/medium/high).</li>
<li>Current leadership style (predominantly transformational / transactional / mixed).</li>
<li>Current balance exploration-exploitation.</li>
<li>Apakah leadership style fit dengan environment? Jika tidak, apa rekomendasi?</li>
</ul>
Gunakan Jansen et al. framework untuk sistematis analysis.</p>
</div>
