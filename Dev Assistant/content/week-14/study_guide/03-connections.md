## Minggu 14 dalam Peta Mata Kuliah (Capstone)

Minggu 14 adalah <strong>capstone</strong>—mengintegrasikan banyak tema dari 13 minggu sebelumnya. Lihat dan pelajari matriks di bawah sebagai peta jalan untuk UAS preparation.

| Minggu | Bagaimana Minggu 14 Mengintegrasikan |
|--------|--------------------------------------|
| 1 | Definisi strategi—dalam hypercompetition, strategi = continuous adaptation |
| 2 | Leadership penting di semua 5 tahap proses; Minggu 14 memperdalam strategic leadership |
| 3 | Mason 2007 complexity—hypercompetition adalah complexity extreme |
| 4 | Dynamic capabilities Teece = exploration capability di Jansen framework |
| 5–6 | Temporary advantage = strategi generik + supplementing moves yang terus bergerak |
| 7 | Tailoring ke industry lifecycle: mature → hypercompetition (shakeout) transitions |
| 8 | International hypercompetition: GoTo vs. Grab (regional scale) |
| 9 | Diversifikasi sebagai hedge against hypercompetition di single industry |
| 10 | ESG sebagai differentiator ketika product differentiation sulit dipertahankan |
| 11 | Eksekusi dan culture—ambidextrous leaders build culture untuk exploration+exploitation |
| 12 | Business model innovation sebagai mechanism creating temporary advantages |
| 13 | Stakeholder engagement = cooperative advantage yang sulit direplikasi di hypercompetition |

### Empat Hubungan Konseptual Kunci (Capstone-Level)

1. **Hypercompetition ↔ Temporary Advantage.</strong> Traditional strategic management (Porter Five Forces, RBV) mengasumsikan <em>sustainable</em> advantage. Hypercompetition framework mengingatkan: mungkin yang realistic adalah <em>sequence of temporary advantages</em>. Ini menuntut dynamic capabilities (Minggu 4) dan continuous business model innovation (Minggu 12).
2. **TMT Integration ↔ Execution.</strong> Kumar-Pansari engagement (Minggu 13) dan Kaplan-Norton execution (Minggu 11) konvergen pada pentingnya senior leadership alignment. Chen, Lin &amp; Michel (2010) menambahkan: <em>integration</em> adalah prasyarat effective aggressive action.
3. **Exploration ↔ Innovation Capability.</strong> Exploration March (1991) = business model innovation (Minggu 12) + dynamic capabilities (Minggu 4) + culture of experimentation (Mason 2007 learning organization). Perusahaan yang low exploration accumulate technical debt dan menjadi rigid.
4. **Leadership Style ↔ Environment.</strong> Jansen et al. (2009): tidak ada single best leadership style. Di dynamic environments, transformational (exploration-enabling) lebih valuable; di stable environments, transactional (exploitation-enabling) cukup. Contextual leadership.
